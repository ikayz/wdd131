About BudgetNest

BudgetNest was created to help people build better financial habits through simple tracking and goal-setting. The project began as a learning exercise and has grown into a prototype that prioritizes usability and clarity. We believe money management should be simple and friendly — not scary.

Our mission

Make budgeting approachable for everyday people. We focus on clear visuals, gentle nudges, and small, practical features that actually help users save and plan.

Who we are

A small team of designers and developers who care about financial inclusion and usable interfaces. We're experimenting with small, privacy-first tools that give people control over their finances without selling their data.

Roadmap & priorities

  • Improved import connectors for more banks in different regions
  • Shared budgets for households and roommates
  • More in-app insights and automated savings rules
